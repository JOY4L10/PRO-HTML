Welcome to KATTAPPANA

About My Hometown

Kattappana is a beautiful place known for its rich culture, history, and natural beauty.

Attractions

  • Historical Monuments: Visit the famous historical sites that tell the story of our town.
  • Parks & Gardens: Enjoy nature at our scenic parks and botanical gardens.
  • Local Markets: Experience the vibrant street markets with delicious food and handicrafts.

Events & Festivals

Every year, Kattappana hosts exciting cultural festivals and celebrations, including:

  • Sports Tournaments
  • Fests
  • Food Carnival

Famous Food

Our town is known for its delicious local dishes such as:

  • Kappa Biriyani
  • Pazhampori & Beef
  • Macroni
